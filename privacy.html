Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 1, 2026

CheapTents.com ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

CheapTents.com is a static informational website. We do not require registration, do not collect personal information directly (such as names, email addresses, or payment details), and do not have user accounts or login systems.

However, certain information may be collected automatically when you visit our site:

  • Log data: Our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may collect standard server log information including your IP address, browser type, referring page, pages visited, and the date and time of your visit.
  • Analytics data: We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. This collects anonymized data about page views, traffic sources, device types, and general geographic location. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your web browser. Our site may use the following types of cookies:

  • Analytics cookies: Set by Google Analytics to distinguish unique visitors and track site usage patterns. These cookies do not identify you personally.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some websites you visit.

Our Amazon affiliate links use the Amazon Associates program. When you purchase a product through our links, Amazon tracks the referral and may pay us a commission. This transaction occurs entirely on Amazon's platform and is governed by Amazon's terms and privacy policy.

Our site uses Google Fonts to display typography. When you load a page, your browser connects to Google's servers to download font files. Google may collect your IP address and browser information in connection with this request. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Because we do not collect personal data directly, the risk of a data breach from our site is minimal. However, no internet transmission is completely secure. We encourage you to use appropriate security measures when browsing any website.
